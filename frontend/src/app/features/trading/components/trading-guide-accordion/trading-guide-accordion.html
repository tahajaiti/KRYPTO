How does Trading Work?

Market vs Limit

Limit orders wait until another player takes the opposite side at your exact price (or better). Market orders ignore your limit price and buy/sell instantly at whatever prices other players are currently offering.

Status: OPEN

If your order says OPEN, it means it is sitting in the order book. This happens when there's no one actively taking the opposite side of your trade at your requested price yet. Just wait, or cancel it and try a different price.

Status: CANCELLED

If a MARKET order gets cancelled instantly, it means nobody is trading that coin right now. Since there's zero liquidity, the system cancels your trade so your funds don't get stuck in a black hole.
